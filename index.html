All-inclusive Arctic VIP tours

Our team of dedicated professionals have extensive experience from all aspects of life in the arctic region. One of the many advantages we offer is that our team includes true Sami people, the original "Laplanders". This people live in this region since thousands of years, and are the only ones having unrestricted rights of movement, as well as reindeer ownership and herding. Through our unique connection to these original tribes, we are able to offer unsurpassed flexibility, sights and activities normally not available, and most of all: perfect knowledge of the region.
